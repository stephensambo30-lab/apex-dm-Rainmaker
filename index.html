Process

How the operation runs

From first DM to booked call. Repeatable. Scalable. Data-driven.

01

Offer Foundation Check

Before we write a single message, we run three checks: offer clarity, market-offer fit, and results proof. No amount of DM skill fixes a bad offer. We check the foundation first.

02

Dual-Layer PDOCS Research

Market-level research to understand root problems, desires, and obstacles. Individual prospect research to mine their exact language. We never write openers from memory — we write them from data.

03

Knowledge Gap Analysis

We identify the specific gap between what the market thinks they know and what's actually keeping them stuck. That gap becomes the backbone of every conversation in the niche.

04

Signal-Based Outreach

How Hero openers. Emotional mirror openers. Signal intelligence reads. 15–20 hyper-personalized outreach messages per session. Nothing mass. Everything intentional.

05

EAACO Conversation Execution

Full five-stage framework. Human Reasoning Stack runs before every reply. Prospect closes themselves — we just surface the contradictions and create permission.

06

Obligation + Show Rate Protection

Four-touch pre-call sequence. Loom video resource. "One thing you want to walk away with" question. These three moves alone take show rate from 60% to 85%+.
